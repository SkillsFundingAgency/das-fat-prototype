Cookies on this service

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a service.

We use cookies to store information about how you use this service, such as the pages you visit.

We deliver this service over a set of subdomains.

Your cookie preferences on this service apply to the following domains:

  • findapprenticeshiptraining.apprenticeships.education.gov.uk
  • find-epao.apprenticeships.education.gov.uk
  • beta-login.apprenticeships.education.gov.uk

Cookie settings

We use 2 types of cookie. You can choose which cookies you’re happy for us to use.

Cookies that measure website use

We use Google Analytics to measure how you use this service so we can improve it based on user needs. We do not allow Google to use or share the data about how you use this service.

Google Analytics sets cookies that store anonymised information about:

  • how you got to the service
  • the pages you visit on GOV.UK and government digital services, and how long you spend on each page
  • what you click on while you’re visiting the service

Cookies may be set by other third-party services to do things like measure how you view YouTube videos that are on the service.

If you have an account with other Education and Skills Funding Agency services, the information we collect using analytics cookies will link to your account. This will help us make the service work better for you.

Strictly necessary cookies

These essential cookies remember:

  • your progress through a form (for example a licence application)
  • the notifications you’ve seen so we do not show them to you again

They always need to be on.
